Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how mobile apps published by Hiromu Izuwa handle information when you use them on iOS or Android.

Information handled by the apps

Some apps may store learning progress, scores, preferences, or similar app data locally on your device so the app can work as expected.

Hiromu Izuwa does not require you to create an account to use the apps covered by this policy unless an app clearly states otherwise.

Advertising

Some apps display ads using Google AdMob. AdMob and its partners may process information such as device information, advertising identifiers, app interactions, approximate location derived from network information, and other data needed to provide, measure, and improve ads, subject to your device settings and consent choices.

Where required, an app may ask for tracking permission or show privacy and consent choices before requesting ads.

Third-party services

Advertising and consent features may be provided by Google services. Their handling of information is governed by their own policies.

Google Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy

Your choices

On iOS, you can manage tracking permission in Settings. On Android, you can manage available advertising and privacy controls in your device settings. Where an app provides a privacy options control, you can use it to review available advertising consent choices.

Children

The apps are not intended to knowingly collect personal information directly from children. If you believe that personal information has been provided in error, please contact us.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated when apps, services, or legal requirements change. The effective date above will be updated when changes are made.

Contact
